Zugspitze is called *Top of Germany*, it's 2962 meters elevation. We have planed a hike for a long time, eventually the summer is coming, so we could mike it true.

We purchased two tickets offline, about 78 euro per person. It's a little expensive, but I think it's worth. The whole trip includes 4 step. We took a mini train first, it across over 4 kilometers tunnel from Eibsee to the sightseeing platform. We took a rest and had a simple lunch at a vegan restaurant. The ice wasn't melt and the scenery was fantastic. Then we took cable car to the top peak of Zugspitze. Form the top, the Eibsee looks like a blue gemstone on the earth. In the end, we took cable car back to the Eibsee. I have taken lots of photos using my camera, it was my unforgettable memory.
