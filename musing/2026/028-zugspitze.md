Zugspitze is called *Top of Germany*, it's 2962 meters elevation. We have planed a hike for a long time, eventually the summer is coming, so we can mike it true.

We purchased two tickets offline, it's 78 euro per person. It's a little expensive, but I think it's worth. The whole trip includes 4 step. We took a mini train first, it across over 4 kilometers tunnel from Eibsee to the sightlight platform. We took a rest and had a simple lunch at a vegan restaurant. The ice wasn't melt and the scenary was fantastic. Then we took cable car
