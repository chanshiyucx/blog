_"Seize the day, gather ye rosebuds while ye may."_ I'm quite fond of this line. There's something about its poetic richness that conveys a beautifully humanistic philosophy of life, so I decided to feature it in my blog's bio.

I discovered it comes from the poem "To the Virgins, to Make Much of Time."
