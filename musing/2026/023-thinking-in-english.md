Today I listened to an episode that talked about how to start thinking in English. It has always been the biggest challenge for me to stop thinking in my native language first. This episode gave six sentence starters that I can use throughout my day: I want, I need, I love, I hate, I think, and I feel. Using these six sentence starters every day will help me think in English more fluently. I also think this is a simple start. I can make it part of my daily routine, so why not give it a try?
