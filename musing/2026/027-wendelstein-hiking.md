I broke my hiking record yesterday when we climbed Wendelstein. The weather was bad because the wind was cold, and I was only wearing a T-shirt. At one point, we even decided to go back. But we felt like we would regret it if we just turned around, so we chose to finish it in the end. We spent about two hours getting to the summit. It was over 900 meters of elevation gain, and it broke my record.

Near the peak, we saw a few chamois. A local told us they're rarely seen and considered a sign of good luck. What a lucky day!
