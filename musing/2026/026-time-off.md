The company I work for now is a subsidiary of a company headquartered in China. Most of my colleagues in the office are Chinese. I've found that, compared with local European colleagues, Chinese people are less likely to request time off. They tend to be more careful and cautious, whereas European colleagues are more casual and don't care as much about what other people think. This is why Chinese companies in Europe prefer to hire Chinese people.
