Chinese people are always applying for different visas throughout their lives. It sounds funny, but it's certainly true. Whether it's for work or travel, you need to apply for a valid visa first. It's really inconvenient. You have to fill out various forms and pay a number of fees, then spend a couple of weeks waiting for an answer. If the application is refused, it really sucks.
