Chiemsee is called the Bavarian Sea. It's the biggest lake in Bavaria. We had a nice hike last Saturday. In the middle of the lake, there are two famous islands. The bigger one is called Herreninsel, and the smaller one is called Fraueninsel. I'm curious about why they have these two names. There is a palace on Herreninsel and a monastery on Fraueninsel. After spending a whole day walking around the two islands, I certainly found Fraueninsel more interesting. Spring was starting, and the flowers were blooming. Besides, we also spent about two hours taking a tour around the whole lake. Feeling the wind on the boat was an impressive experience.
