I had a sudden idea to add a screenshot of my blog's home page, just to make the [zero](https://github.com/chanshiyucx/zero) theme more obvious for visitors. So I opened Figma right away. I'm a total newbie with Figma and had only used it once before to design my blog's favicon. You can see it in the browser tab.

I told Grok what I needed and asked it to walk me through the steps, but it didn't work out. The steps it gave were just too complicated for me to follow. So I switched to Gemini. It gave me something simpler and more doable. When I finally got the finished [image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/chanshiyucx/zero/refs/heads/master/doc/screenshot/theme.png), I felt a real sense of accomplishment. I think I'm starting to like Figma.
